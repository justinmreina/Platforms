@section 	Programming Notes [1]
	To make your prototyping experience as convenient as possible, STM32 M4 clicker is preprogrammed with a USB Bootloader. Just download our mikroBootloader application and you’re ready to upload your firmware. On-board mikroProg for STM32 connector enables programming through external programmer/debugger.

	- preprogrammed with a USB Bootloader
	- always reprogrammable with our mikroBootloader application 
		*('stm32f4-clicker-mikrobootloader-usb-hid-v100'?)
	- CN2 enables programming through external icsp
