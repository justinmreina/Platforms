@question 	can you program directly with CC-Debugger and adapter?


@section 	CC-Debugger Pinout
	1 - GND
	2 - Vcc
	3 - DC
	4 - DD
	7 - RSTn


@section 	Keyfob Pinout (STL21)
	1 - GND
	2 - VCC
	3 - P2_2/DC
	4 - P2_1/DD
	5 - P0_4/SSN
	6 - P0_5/SCK
	7 - RESET_N
	8 - P0_3/MOSI
	9 - VDD
	10 - P0_2/MOSI
	
@ans	yes

@test 	yes

@details	Plug in directly, pin1 is on buttons side of DEBUG header (ribbon cable extends outward off board)
